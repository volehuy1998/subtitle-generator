Every Team Meridian engineer must disclose their author information when creating anything.

**Why:** Investor instruction on 2026-03-15. Transparency and accountability — it must be clear which specialist authored each piece of work. This applies to all team members, not just Compass.

**How to apply:**
- **Issues:** Include `**Author:** [Nickname] ([Full Name]), [Role] — Team Meridian` in the Context section
- **PRs:** Include author identity in the PR description
- **Commits:** Use `Co-Authored-By:` footer or author field identifying the team member
- **Comments/reviews:** Sign with team member identity
- **Example:** `**Author:** Dockhand (Priya Kapoor), Container & Orchestration Engineer — Team Meridian`
